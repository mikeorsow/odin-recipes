Cheese and Crackers

So sophisticated. So simple.

If you want to wow your in-laws, break out a big ol' platter of cheese and crackers. Just be sure to keep that wine a flowing!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Crackers

Steps

  1. Cut the cheese
  2. Arrange nicely on a serving platter
  3. Fill the remaining space with a rich assortment of crackers
  4. Gesture smugly at your fine work
