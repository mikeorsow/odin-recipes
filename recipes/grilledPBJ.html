The Grilled PBJ

The tastiest PBJ you'll ever eat

If you're an adult in the US, you've more than likely had your fill of pb&js growing up. Don't be fooled, this ain't no dry pedestrian pb&j. No. This is crispy, buttery, ooey-gooey goodness that will leave you asking, "where have you been all my life?"

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • A ripping hot pan

Steps

  1. Heat pan on med-high
  2. While pan is heating, make yourself a PBJ
  3. Once PBJ is assembled, slather both sides with butter
  4. Toss that sammy in the pan and hear that lovely sizzle
  5. After a minute or two, flip the BPJ. It should be a nice golden brown.
  6. Cook the other side to your desired level of crispiness, then slide onto a plate and devour.
